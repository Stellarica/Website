What is Stellarica?

Stellarica is a open world space exploration game where you can go around the world exploring. There are many planets that you can go around inside of a starship that you can control. This starship will be the guide to all of your explorations!

What is a starship?


You need a starship to start flying around! Without a starship/rocket you wont get anywhere. You can create your own starship/rocket and design it to your likings.

What are the intentions of the game?


Stellarica's intentions are to be as science-fiction themed as possible. This means that most stuff inside the game will most likely be a thing in real life!
Stellarica wants to be a exploration game inside of minecraft allowing players to travel between distant planets,build their own starships and much more to come!
